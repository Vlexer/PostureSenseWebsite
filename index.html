How does PostureSense work?

PostureSense uses your device's camera to monitor your posture in real-time. When it detects that you're slouching, it sends a gentle reminder notification to help you maintain better posture. The app runs in the background and doesn't store any video data.

How much does PostureSense cost?

PostureSense is completely FREE! We believe good posture should be accessible to everyone. There are no hidden fees or premium features - everything is included in the free version.

Is PostureSense secure?

Yes, PostureSense is designed with privacy in mind. All posture processing happens locally on your device - no video or image data is ever sent to our servers. The app works offline and doesn't require an internet connection.

Does PostureSense work if I have a standing desk?

Yes! PostureSense can monitor your posture whether you're sitting or standing. It adapts to your desk setup and can help you maintain proper alignment in both positions.
